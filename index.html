About Us

🌍 Vision

To create a world where every person has access to quality clothing, regardless of their financial circumstances. We envision communities where generosity and sustainability go hand in hand.

🤝 Mission

GiveBack SA is committed to collecting, organizing, and distributing clothing donations to those in need. We believe that clothing is a fundamental need and that everyone deserves dignity and access to appropriate attire.

Who We Are

GiveBack SA is a community-driven initiative focused on helping individuals and families through clothing donations.

Our Impact

  • Helping low-income families access quality clothing
  • Supporting children's homes and care facilities
  • Promoting sustainability through clothing reuse
  • Building stronger communities through giving

Our Values

  • Compassion: We care about those we serve
  • Integrity: We operate with honesty and transparency
  • Community: We believe in collective action
  • Sustainability: We promote environmental responsibility
